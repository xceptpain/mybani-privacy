# Privacy Policy for My Bani

**Last Updated: February 7, 2026**

## Introduction

My Bani ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Reading Progress**: We store your reading progress locally on your device to help you continue where you left off.
- **Bookmarks**: Any bookmarks you create are stored locally on your device.
- **Preferences**: Your app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Analytics**: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience.
- **Advertising Data**: For users of the free version, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information as described in their privacy policy.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
Our free version displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data for advertising purposes. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Apple Services
We use Apple's StoreKit for processing in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies to these transactions: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Data Storage

All your personal data (reading progress, bookmarks, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store this information on external servers.

## Premium Subscription

My Bani offers a premium subscription that removes advertisements and provides additional features. Subscription purchases are processed by Apple and subject to Apple's terms and conditions.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app contains religious scripture content suitable for all ages.

## Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can:
- Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
- Clear your reading progress and bookmarks within the app settings

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.
